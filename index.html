About US

Ascetic Ascent is a Catholic contemplative and active association of the faithful initiated . out of the vision of Kevin Tower, a Marian Catechist and Lay Carmelite. Falling into the depths of the darkness of the prison cell, Kevin found Jesus to be the one true and consistent light that would always reach him. Kevin developed a personal relationship with Jesus that set his heart on fire with Divine love filling him with Faith, Hope and Love. From his cell the Lord has steadily and increasingly guided Kevin in the Divine Life. Kevin was formed spiritually by authentic Catholic men and woman and the writings of Saints such as Saint John of the Cross and Teresa of Avila. The Ascetic Ascent movement is about empowerment and multiplication of authentic Catholic men and women witnessing and ministering to one another. Jesus has called you and is calling you out of the darkness and into the light. Jesus leads you into the footsteps of peace! Let the Holy Spirit empower and strengthen you today!

Come join us and bring your family and friends.
